About

I am Md Sanowar Hossain, a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at the University of North Dakota. My research focuses on photonic integrated circuits, silicon photonics, reconfigurable PICs, optical signal processing, and machine learning-assisted photonics. My background includes silicon nitride optical filter design, microwave photonics, photonic device simulation, and layout development using tools such as Lumerical, GDSFactory, and IPKISS.
